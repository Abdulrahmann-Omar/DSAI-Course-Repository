### Added
- **Repository Structure**: Complete 4-year curriculum organization
- **Year 1**: Foundation courses (Programming, Digital Logic, Calculus, Linear Algebra)
- **Year 2**: Core courses (Data Structures, Databases, ML, Algorithms)
- **Year 3**: Advanced courses (Deep Learning, NLP, CV, XAI, Information Theory)

- **Supporting files**: README.md, LICENSE, CONTRIBUTING.md, requirements.txt
